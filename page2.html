Travel

Below is a list of one process you could take to get to Antigua and Barbuda.

    1. Go to your airport

    2. Get on a flight that takes you straight to Antigua and Barbuda

    3. Get a taxi to your hotel

    4. Enjoy your vacation!

Basics about traveling

Some taxi drivers will try and charge you extra money thinking you won't understand how the currency works! Watch out!

Another thing to watch out for are pickpocketers! This is the most common crime in Antigua.

Good news about traveling here is that the tourist crime rate is low in comparions to other neighbooring islands!
